Maglabs Stratum (MLS) is a cloud-based, secure and scalable asset management platform enabling the rapid development of content management solutions for a business.

allows for distribution and workflow support so you can manage brand and media assets out of the box whilst customising the UI to support business processes.

Costa Coffee
Managing brand assets globally
MagLabs created the Costa Resource Centre (CRC) that co-ordinates brand assets, communications, audits and transactions globally across 3,000 stores in 40 countries.
The CRC was developed to support Costa Coffee’s ambition to continue to grow on a global scale while maintaining controlling brand consistency and content distribution.
It contains the latest campaign artwork, store designs, operational manuals and fresh menu ideas are some of the assets and so much more. Built using responsive design, the CRC also manages key activities ranging from video streaming of training materials and supply chain order processing, to hour-by-hour tracking and auditing of franchise store revenues around the world, whilst simultaneously communicating to their global finance system.

A dedicated Maglabs service team supports the CRC.

Marks & Spencer
Clothing & Home Packaging Hub (C&H Packaging Hub).
Maglabs created the C&H Packaging Hub a central library which controls all of the corporate packaging and labelling, ensuring global legal and brand compliance for domestic and international markets. The system allows users to manage in excess of 4,000 templates that produce an estimated 1.5 billion tickets, tags, and labels in multiple languages. Updates to C&H Packaging Hub have included adding sizing information for different regions and regional guide pricing for packaging and labelling items.
Dedicated Team
A dedicated Maglabs service team supports C&H Packaging Hub and works closely with the C&H Packaging team who maintain live key information for label and tickets as well as supplier details. The result is a central portal for faster, more efficient and accurate label & ticket production.

Telemundo Internacional Media (Ti.media).
MagLabs created Ti.media a cloud hosted digital asset management solution that manages and distributes the full suite of Telemundo Internacional’s marketing assets, including; TV spots, trailers, images, brand guidelines, and more.
Ti.media is branded to include associated banners and title images for each TV show and is cloud hosted to ensure accelerated transfer speeds for large trailers and images. Access to content on Ti.media is strictly controlled to ensure that Telemundo’s clients have access to the right content at the right time.
Migration of Assets
Maglabs assisted in migrating Telemundo’s assets from their incumbent supplier to ensure a smooth transition with no system downtime. This new solution has a modern design and an intuitive user interface that allows users to browse content faster, and reduces asset upload time by utilising bulk upload functionality.

Manchester United Football Club Licensing (MUFC Licensing)
MagLabs worked with Manchester United to automate brand enterprise merchandising operations. Over the past 8 years, MUFC Licensing has become a centralised global workflow process that manages Manchester United’s global merchandising and licensing operations. This end-to-end system includes everything from the deal memo and contract creation, to product approvals, invoicing, and financial and royalty reporting.
Through workflow automation, MUFC Licensing has significantly improved efficiency and business processes, reducing costs, whilst seamlessly integrating with finance related business systems.

Sargasso

Sargasso is based on the powerful ML Stratum Platform.
It is a flexible, off-the-shelf, web-enabled product that securely organises and manages a business’s digital assets and empowers teams globally to collaborate on projects giving them access to branding tools and files both in the office and on the go.
Teams across the business get access as they need it, thanks to powerful and flexible metadata and permissions structure, as well as user friendly interface across all devices. It’s customisable user interface to match company’s visual identity.
Sargasso offers cost-optimised and secure cloud storage. Moreover, enterprise operations are streamlined by seamlessly connecting Sargasso with other tools within the company ecosystem.
Businesses get more value from their creative assets.
